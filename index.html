About the Game

MatchMania is a fun and challenging memory game where you match pairs of cards within a limited time.
Test your memory and beat your best score!

How to Play

  • Flip cards to find matching pairs.
  • Match all pairs before time runs out.
  • Try to beat your best score!
  • If you think you're good enough, try CRAZY MODE!
